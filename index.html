2026-2027 Enacted Budget — Housing Review Exemptions

🎯 SEQR Reform Overview

The 2026-2027 NY State Budget (TED Part S) introduces significant exemptions from SEQR review for housing projects.

Exemption Thresholds:

  • Urbanized Areas: Up to 300 units exempt
  • Non-Urban Areas: Up to 100 units exempt
  • Unzoned Areas: Up to 20 units exempt

Requirements:

  • Must be on previously disturbed land
  • Existing water/sewer connections required
  • Cannot be in exclusively industrial zones

EIS Timeline:

1 year for initial determination, 2 years max to finalize EIS.
