🎯 SEQR Reform Overview

The 2026-2027 NY State Budget (TED Part S) exempts housing from SEQR review based on unit count thresholds. Projects must be on previously disturbed land with existing water/sewer.

Exemption Thresholds:

  • Urbanized: Up to 300 units
  • Non-Urban: Up to 100 units
  • Unzoned: Up to 20 units

Parcel Colors:

  • Green: Likely qualifies (developed + has water/sewer)
  • Orange: Has water/sewer but undeveloped land
  • Red: No water/sewer — unlikely to qualify
  • Gray: Exempt/institutional — check eligibility

EIS Timeline:

1 year for initial determination, 2 years max to finalize.
